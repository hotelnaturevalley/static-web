Welcome to Nature Valley

A Himalayan Sanctuary of Peace & Comfort

Hotel Nature Valley was formed by like-minded entrepreneurs with a passion for travel and exploration. Our fundamental values are service, design, and simplicity — when you reserve a room with us, you're guaranteed to have an outstanding experience.

Nestled in a forested area near Dalhousie, our tranquil retreat provides a serene setting for a rejuvenating getaway. Being surrounded by snow-capped mountains mesmerises the senses and rejuvenates weary spirits. Located at the western extremity of the Dhauladhar mountain range, we're well-known for our moderate climate and natural beauty.
